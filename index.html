Exploratory Data Analysis¶

Dividing the data based on platform where 3DS is The Nintendo 3DS,

DC is The Dreamcast, DS is The Nintendo DS or "Developers' System",

GB is The Game Boy, GBA is The Game Boy Advance, GC is The GameCube,

N64 is The Nintendo 64,

PC is a Personal Computer,

PS is The PlayStation, PS2 is The PlayStation 2, PS3 is The PlayStation 3, PS4 is The PlayStation 4,

PSP is The PlayStation Portable, PSV is The PlayStation Vita,

WS is The WonderSwan,

Wii is The Wii, WiiU is The Wii U,

X360 is The Xbox 360, XB is The Xbox, and XOne is The Xbox One:
